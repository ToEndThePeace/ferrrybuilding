Marketplace Services

Shopping

Numerous shops, large and small, celebrate food in all its forms, offering everything from artisan cheeses, organic produce, sweets, baked goods, and the freshest of local fish and meats.

Cafés & Restaurants

Two spectacular restaurants anchor the bayside corners of the Ferry Building with sweeping views of the waterfront. Numerous cafés provide casual meals that reflect the bounty of the daily market. Day and night, whether sipping a morning cappucino or an evening glass of wine, guests enjoy an incomparable window to the life of the City.

Shipping

If you are traveling light by plane, train or boat and find you want to take a bit of artisan San Francisco home with you, individual shops have shipping options available.

For details, inquire at info@ferrybuildingmarketplace.com.

Private Events

Located on the waterfront, The Ferry Building offers a unique site for special events, weddings and corporate receptions.
