Rancho Gordo to Open New Store

Rancho Gordo New World Specialty Food, a Napa-based specialty food company has announced plans to open a retail space at the San Francisco Ferry Plaza Building Marketplace, home of Bay Area food legends like Acme Bread, Cowgirl Creamery and the Slanted Door. Read More

Cowgirl Creamery Experience

Cowgirl Creamery, award winning makers of artisan cheese and proprietors of three of the best cheese stores in the country, are offering a 30 minute Cowgirl Creamery Experience at their shop in San Francisco’s landmark Ferry Building. Read More

Mariposa to Open Marketplace Shop

San Francisco’s Ferry Building announced today that Mariposa Baking Company will move from its kiosk location into one the coveted Marketplace nave shops. Mariposa’s mission is to create gluten-free baked goods that are indiscernible from the finest, high-caliber gluten-based products available. Read More

Prather Ranch Lassoes Larger Space

Prather Ranch Meat Company, a Bay Area company with a well established reputation for selling high quality, humanely raised meat products such as dry-aged beef, heritage breed pork and lamb, American bison and local poultry, is excited to announce it is expanding its operations. Read More

Market Bar Debuts Weekday Breakfast

MarketBar opened at the Ferry Building Marketplace in 2003 and has had a steady following for its lunch, dinner, drinks, and weekend brunch offerings. Built on a seasonal menu of fresh fare from local farmers and purveyors, owner Doug Biederbeck said he is proud to announce the new weekday Breakfast Menu. Read More

Wagon Wheel Cheese Debuts at Cowgirl Creamery

Cowgirl Creamery debuts Wagon Wheel, the latest addition to their lineup. The cheese makers refer to this as an “everyday” cheese. The twenty-five pound wheel is made with milk from the Cowgirl Creamery’s West Marin neighbor–Straus Family Creamery. Read More
